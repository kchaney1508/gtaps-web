Development Brief

Build v1.0.0
Last Update March 1st, 2026
Next Update Late March-April
Previous Objective We just finished the website and Discord bot, which locked in the public-facing site, base layout and the first-pass onboarding flow.
Current Objective Right now, we're focused on building the in-game framework, policing system, callouts and importing assets. This can be a lengthy process and could take up to three or more months.
Content and Assets This includes vehicles, buildings, texture changes and audio files. We also need to ensure everything complies with Rockstar's ToS while keeping the experience fun.
Character Framework Our foundation for player data such as characters and saved settings. Once stable, we'll be able to expand our Mobile Data Terminal without breaking everything each update.
Callouts and Policing Covers callouts, traffic stops, dispatch flow, AI behaviors and report handling. The goal is believable scenarios that stay smooth and fair.
